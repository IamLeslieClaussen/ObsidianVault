The first Step is write a Research Brief:
1. The research Goal 
2. The research questions: Not the questions that you would ask the interviewee. These are the questions that you would like to have the answers to. 
3. The Research method. 

Interviews:
Often enough to interview 5 people to cover most the questions that are needed.


How to conduct [[User Research ]] Interviews:
1. First define screening requirements
  - Find people that fit target audience. 
2. Write an interview guide (Always keep the goal in mind and form the key questions.)
