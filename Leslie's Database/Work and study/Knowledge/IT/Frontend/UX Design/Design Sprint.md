A time bound process, with 5 phases typically spread over full 8-hour days.

Goal
Solve a critical design challenge through designing, prototyping, and testing ideas with users.


A Design Sprint is a proven methodology for solving problems through designing, prototyping and testing ideas with users. Design Sprints quickly align teams under a shared vision with clearly defined goals and deliverables. It is a tool for developing a hypothesis, prototyping an idea, and testing it rapidly with as little investment as possible in an environment as possible. 


Design Sprint Methodology 
it has six phases [[Understand]], [[Define]], [[Sketch]], [[Decide]], [[Prototype]] and [[Validate]]
